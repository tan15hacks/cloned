# Hearthvale v3.5 — Living World and Character Animation

## Character animation

The geometric cartoon characters now animate without sprite sheets:

- Four-direction walking with arm and leg movement
- Idle breathing and blinking
- Hoe, watering can, axe, pickaxe, seed, fishing rod, sword, and consumable action poses
- Rain umbrellas for residents
- Existing combat hit flashing, knockback, invulnerability, and attack arcs remain compatible

## NPC schedules

All 18 named residents have time-based routines. NPCs move between work, gardens, plazas, shops, training spaces, inns, gates, and the observatory. Rain and snow alter ordinary schedules, while Sora continues the night observatory routine.

## Living settlements

Hearthvale Village and Silvercrest City now contain background citizens following lightweight walking circuits. Citizens shelter during rain or snow. Service buildings show OPEN or CLOSED signs, follow individual business hours, and safe settlements illuminate at night.

## Interiors

Two lightweight separate interior maps are included:

- Farmhouse: bed, storage, journal, hearth, furniture, and exit
- Adventurers' Guild: service counter, bounty board, training notes, resident guild staff, furniture, and exit

The interior mode does not generate or render the 57,344-tile overworld while the player is indoors.

## Friendship and dialogue

Dialogue now responds to time, weather, current NPC activity, and friendship. Milestones at 3, 6, and 9 friendship unlock journal events and small gifts. Existing Chapter 1 dialogue hooks remain installed after the living-world layer so Mira and Guildmaster Aria still advance the guided opening chapter.

## Save compatibility

Existing v1–v3 browser saves receive a living-world state automatically. Saves made inside an interior reopen safely in the overworld, while friendship, equipment, Chapter 1, guild, cave, farm, and inventory progress remain intact.
